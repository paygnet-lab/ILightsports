About Us

I am a FIFA Licensed Football Agent and a Registered Football administration with The Football Association (English FA), as well as the Founder & Director of iLight Sports Management.

With a background in law and business administration, I bring a professional, strategic, and ethical approach to player representation. My experience enables me to understand the legal, commercial, and personal aspects of a footballer's career, supporting players in making informed decisions both on and off the pitch.

Through iLight Sports Management, I provide player representation, career management, talent identification, and football advisory services. I am committed to building trusted relationships with players, coaches, clubs, academies, and football stakeholders while creating opportunities for long-term career development.

My vision is to establish a respected football management company that delivers integrity, professionalism, and meaningful pathways for talented players in the UK and internationally.
